Our Story

We are a family-owned restaurant located in the heart of the city, and we take pride in serving authentic Italian cuisine made with the freshest and finest ingredients. Our story began over 50 years ago, when our family moved to the city and brought with them their passion for cooking.

Over the years, we have had the privilege of serving some of the most well-known people from history, including presidents, actors, musicians, and more.Our restaurant has become a landmark in the city, and we are proud to continue serving our community with the same love and passion for food that we started with.

Come and dine with us to experience the taste of Italy, and see why our restaurant has been a favorite of so many throughout the years.
